# Sales-Performance-Dashboard(Interactive Dashboard creation using Power BI)

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance using Power BI. The dashboard helps monitor overall business performance by providing insights into total sales, profit, quantity sold, monthly sales trends, payment methods, product performance, and category-wise sales. It enables businesses to make data-driven decisions and improve overall sales performance.

## Dataset used
- <a href="https://github.com/PIKKILIMANOHAR/Sales-Performance-Dashboard/blob/main/Sales_Dataset.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
- What is the total sales generated?
- What is the total quantity of products sold?
- What is the total profit earned?
- What is the overall profit percentage?
- How do sales and profit vary across different months?
- Which payment mode contributes the highest sales?
- Which products generate the highest sales?
- Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
- How do daily sales fluctuate throughout the month?
- How do direct sales compare with online sales?

- Dashboard Interaction <a href="https://github.com/PIKKILIMANOHAR/Sales-Performance-Dashboard/blob/main/dashboard_image.png">View Dashboard</a>

## Process
- Collected and imported the sales dataset into Power BI.
- Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query.
- Created DAX measures to calculate Total Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Profit Percentage.
- Designed interactive visualizations to analyze sales across different dimensions.
- Added slicers for Year, Month, Sale Type, and Payment Mode to improve user interaction.
- Generated business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

## Project Insights
- The business generated a total sales value of **366.62K**.
- A total of **132** products were sold during the selected period.
- The overall profit earned was **34K**, resulting in a **10%** profit margin.
- Sales remained consistent throughout the year, with higher sales observed in the last quarter.
- Online and Cash payment modes contributed almost equally to total sales.
- Plastic Mat and Carpet Roll were the top-selling products.
- Personal Care and Household categories generated the highest sales revenue.
- Daily sales analysis showed fluctuations, helping identify peak and low-performing days.
